Color palette

Typography

A grotesk and an italic.

Geist carries every UI surface, headline, and body block. EB Garamond italic is the editorial accent, reserved for the one noun phrase that makes a Hellyeah sentence sound like Hellyeah. Both are on Google Fonts.
