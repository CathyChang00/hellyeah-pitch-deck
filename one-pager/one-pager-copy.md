## 30-Day Engagement

Days 1-5: audit tracking health, creative fatigue, account setup, KPI truth, and first-test scope.

Days 5-15: launch controlled tests, refresh creative, monitor quality, and collect clean evidence.

Days 15-30: increase spend only when tracking is clean, winners repeat, and revenue-side economics hold.

## Scale Rules

Scale budget: KPI inside agreed band, tracking clean, winner creative repeats.

Kill ad: learning spend reached, weak event quality, or CAC/CPI far from target.

Replace creative: CTR/CVR drops, frequency rises, hook fatigue appears, or concept stalls.

Fix tracking: platform ROAS conflicts with revenue-side result, event loss, or MMP mismatch.
