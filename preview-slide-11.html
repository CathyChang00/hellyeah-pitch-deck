Expedia
01
Challenge

The price was wrong before the click

Travel prices moved by the minute, so the price in the ad was stale before a user tapped and did not match checkout. Users bounced and CPA climbed.

02
Move

Unlocked a custom Meta feed that updated itself

A direct Meta API was negotiated for top-tier spend access, then a feed was built to show the real offer, deep-link to the property, and swap creative plus price automatically.

03
Result

Lower cost and restored trust

When every ad matched the real offer, acquisition cost came down and the operating pattern expanded into more projects.
